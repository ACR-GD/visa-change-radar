Experience Malaysia's Digital Nomad Revolution

Tailored Digital Nomad Pass Experience

The exclusive Nomad Pass lets qualified foreign digital professionals stay and work longer in Malaysia — removing tourist visa limits for a more flexible, comfortable work-life experience.

Work-Ready Ecosystem and Curated Lifestyle

Malaysia offers nomad-ready hubs, affordable living, high-speed connectivity, and a safe, productive, comfort, and curated experiences for an ideal work-life balance.

Open Access for Diverse Talents beyond Malaysia

DE Rantau welcomes both tech and non-tech professionals worldwide, offering an easy, globally accessible way to work remotely from Malaysia.

Where work meets wanderlust.
Discover our DE Rantau Malaysia!

DE Rantau is Malaysia's flagship initiative to establish the country as the premier digital nomad hub in Southeast Asia. The programme builds a vibrant ecosystem that enables digital professionals to live, work, and explore Malaysia with access to reliable connectivity, nomad-ready hubs, and tailored services for a productive and fulfilling lifestyle.

At its core is the DE Rantau Nomad Pass, a newly introduced Professional Visit Pass that allows qualified foreign digital nomads to stay and work in Malaysia for up to 12 months. With world-class facilities, curated experiences, and a welcoming community, Malaysia offers the ideal balance of productivity, culture, and adventure for digital nomads from around the globe.

Work, Live & Travel:

Introducing the DE Rantau Nomad Pass

Exclusive pass for inbound digital nomads which eliminates the limitations of a normal tourist pass, so you are free to roam and enjoy your stay while focusing on what matters most – be it your work, content for your channels, places to explore, foods to try, and everything else we can offer here – and trust us, there’s plenty!

Type of Pass:

Professional Visit Pass
(Pas Lawatan Ikhtisas (PLIK))

Features:

• Stay from 3 up to 12 months
• Pass renewable for up to additional 12 months
• Able to bring in spouse & child/children
• Able to bring parent/parents for main pass holder only

Fee:

• MYR 1,000.00 (main)
• MYR 500.00 (dependent)

All you need to know: Digital Nomad Pass

Type of Digital Nomad Pass:

Tech Talent/Profession and Non-Tech Talent/Profession are eligible to apply!

  • Digital Freelancer, Independent Contractor
  • Remote worker (full and part-time) i.e. employee of a company but not required to physically be in the office

Domain of Profession

Tech Talent/Profession:

Digital freelancer, independent contractor, and remote worker who are IT & digital professionals in various domains:

  • Software Engineers
  • Backend Engineers
  • Cloud
  • Cybersecurity
  • Blockchain
  • AI (Artificial Intelligence)
  • Machine Learning (data-related, etc.)
  • Digital Marketing
  • Digital Creative Content
  • Digital Content Development
  • User Experience
  • User Interface
  • Other related positions

Non-Tech Talent/Profession:

  • Chief Executive Officer (Founder/Managing Director/President)
  • Chief Operating Officer
  • Business Development (Growth Manager)
  • Marketing Manager
  • Chief Financial Officer
  • Finance Manager/Accountant
  • Sales Manager
  • Customer Success Manager
  • Human Resources Manager
  • Legal Counsel
  • Public Relation Manager
  • Consultant
  • Customer Service Manager/Customer Service Representative/Customer Success Specialist
  • Communications & Public Relations Manager
  • Administrative Manager
  • Technical Writer
  • Tax Specialist
  • Tax Accountant
  • Production Manager
  • Supply Chain Manager
  • Other any related positions

Proof of Work

Digital Freelancer/Independent Contractor:

  • Active project contract
  • Contract duration > 3 months, can be multiple contracts
  • Clients can be both local & foreign companies

Remote Worker:

  • Active employment contract
  • Contract duration > 3 months
  • Employer is foreign/non-Malaysian based companies

Annual Income

Tech Talent/Profession:

More than USD24,000 per year

Non-Tech Talent/Profession:

More than USD60,000 per year

DE Rantau Nomad Pass:

Digital freelancers/independent contractors are individuals who perform work remotely on a freelance and project basis, with no attachment to a specific company or location.
